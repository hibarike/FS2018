What "Home Alone" about:

A huge family of McCalister is going to go to Christmas in Paris. As usual it happens when they attack, and everything in a hurry begins to gather. Mother and father of eight-year-old Kevin recall that they forgot their youngest son at home. At this time, young Kevin, realizing that he stayed at home himself, decided not to waste time and how to have fun. So that he could return home, the boy does everything he pleases. Anyway, Kevin finds out that a couple of thugs want his house. After all, the boy had to prepare for their arrival.
