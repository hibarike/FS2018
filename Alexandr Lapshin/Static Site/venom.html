What "Venom" about:

Eddie Brock is a well-known journalist and astute reporter who wants to expose the Life Foundation Corporation, headed by the eminent scientist Carlton Drake, who secretly conducts illegal experiments on people, introducing extraterrestrial life forms, symbiots, into their bodies. During a secret visit to the research center, Drake Brock was infected with such a symbiote as a result of the attack of one of the experimental. Shortly afterwards, he begins to observe strange changes in his body, becoming a ruthless and dangerous creature known as "Venom".
