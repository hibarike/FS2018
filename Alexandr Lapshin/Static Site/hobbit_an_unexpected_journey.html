What "The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey" about:

In the film, we see all the characters we have long known and loved by the picture “The Lord of the Rings.” Bilbo Baggins lived quietly and quietly in the Shire until the great magician Gandalf Gray visited him, and then thirteen dwarfs appeared in his house. Together with them, he goes on a long journey to save their kingdom, which was ravaged by a dragon. Their path will pass through the dangerous possessions of orcs and goblins, as well as the brave travelers will face terrible monsters. In one of the tunnels, Bilbo will meet with a creature called Golum. He will also take possession of the ring of Golum, which has extraordinary power, and as it turns out later, with which the fate of all Middle-earth is connected.
