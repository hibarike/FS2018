What "Three Billboards Outside Ebbing, Missouri" about:

More than six months ago, an unknown criminal raped and brutally murdered Angela Hayes near the road leading from their home to the small provincial town of Ebbing, Missouri. No longer able to wait for the results of the stalled investigation, the inconsolable mother rents three billboards located along the ill-fated road and places several phrases there that sharply criticize the local police chief. Despite the fact that billboards are located far from the main highway, this event does not go unnoticed and after some time a bitter confrontation erupts between the embittered woman and the local police.
