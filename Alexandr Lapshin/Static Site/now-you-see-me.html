What "Now You See Me" about:

The film characters Jay, Henley, Jack and Merritt are illusionists who once team up. They give performances on the biggest and most prestigious scenes, shocking the audience with their performances. Their main highlight is the trick with a bank robbery, during which a real crime is committed, but the illusionists themselves do not leave the scene. For the crime committed by the masters of the “illusion of deception”, FBI agent Dylan Rhodes begins the hunt. Incredible trick was just a red herring. In some surprising ways, the guys manage to circle the finger of the FBI and Taddeus Bradley, the most famous exposer of tricks.
