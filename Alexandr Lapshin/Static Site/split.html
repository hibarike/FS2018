What "Split" about:

While the dissociative mental disorders are currently not fully understood and still remain a mystery to modern medicine, doctors still managed to identify several groups of such a rare and sometimes very dangerous mental disorder. One of these groups is the dissociative identity disorder - a rare disease, as a result of which consciousness is divided into a certain number of completely independent parts, which makes it possible for several different people to exist in one body at once. The action film will tell an incredible story about Kevin - a unique person in which there are at least twenty-three personalities. According to the convictions of Dr. Karen Flatcher, a highly qualified specialist in the field of psychology, who, despite his rich professional experience, first encounters a similar phenomenon, Kevin, like other people suffering from this disorder, is able to change his physiology depending on what kind of person this time prevails in his mind. One day, Kevin kidnaps three teenage girls who, having come to their senses, understand with horror that they were captured by the real madman. At that moment, our heroines still did not realize that there was a real nightmare ahead of them, because in the mind of their thief, in addition to completely innocent people, there is a merciless Beast.
