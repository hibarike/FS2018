What "Matrix" about:

The cult sci-fi thriller "The Matrix", filmed by American directors Lana and Andrew Wachowski, appeared on screens in 1999 and marked the beginning of a trilogy. This film is probably one of the few in which a great combination of philosophical idea, fantasy and action. The action picture unfolds in the future. In the center of the plot is a young guy named Thomas Anderson, who leads a double lifestyle. By day, he is the most ordinary employee of a large company, but at night the hacker Neo. Once a strange message arrives on his computer from unknown people “You are stuck in a matrix”. A guy cannot even imagine that this means everything, but soon events begin to occur in his life, thanks to which he will receive answers to all his questions ...
